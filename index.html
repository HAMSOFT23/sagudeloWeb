Who am I?

Samuel Andrés Agudelo was born in the small city of Manizales, Colombia, in the early 2000s.

With a particular like for Game Design, he started developing video games when he was finishing his last year of school; at the age of 17 he joined a game jam and managed to be the designer for a team of 6 without prior experience and getting his first approach to Unity, since then, he has worked with other teams in canceled projects, jam games and personal projects, enhancing his skills as a Game Designer and a Team Worker.

He got fully involved with Unity in 2022 and since then he has been learning the engine and improving his Game Design workflow, personal style, programming and developer skills.
